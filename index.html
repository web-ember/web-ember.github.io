How it works

From camera roll to timeless web story.

The process is designed for normal people, not tech users. Send the memories, approve the vibe, and receive a beautiful link.

Send details

You send photos, names, event type, preferred colors, and the feeling you want the site to have.

We shape story

We organize the moment into sections: hero, timeline, gallery, messages, video, music, or tribute.

Design + build

The page is built mobile-first with smooth animations, emotional visuals, and a clean sharing experience.

Share forever

You receive one beautiful link that can be shared through Instagram, WhatsApp, text, email, or QR code.
